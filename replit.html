Year 11 Replit course materials

In 2021 I started using Replit for Education and in 2022 began using education teams in earnest. I have designed a practical course to cover the outcomes of the HSC Softare Design and Development course with unit testing and markdown lesson plans. It's a work in progress but I'm pretty happy with where it is for Year 11 right now and look forward to updating it for the new curriculum if we ever see it.

Unit 1: Working in Teams

First activity, after learning the basics of input, output, if statements and loops. Students start with a very basic structure of login program and then progress to make it a function, pass parameters and extension with looping and a file for the logins and passwords.
