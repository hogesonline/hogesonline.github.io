Amanda Hogan

Professional Summary

Amanda is a skilled leader of computing education and technology integration in education, dedicated to improving educational outcomes for students and teachers across school environments at local, state and national levels. She accomplishes this through a compassionate and motivating teaching style, direct leadership in the teaching community, input into the development of state curricula, and through producing high-quality, publicly available teaching resources. Amanda is a well-known thought leader in the education community, frequently presenting at both education and technology conferences.

Skill Overview

  • Enthusiastic and experienced teacher, learning designer, teacher trainer, event organiser and public speaker
  • Mentor of team members, peers and junior colleagues to assist others to reach personal goals
  • Passionate about education research and applying research-proven teaching approaches in the classroom
  • Skilled data analyst and programmer in Microsoft Excel, VBA, Python, SQL, Pandas and web technologies.
  • Successful teacher of complex CS concepts to learners from year 3 to a tertiary level, including developing strategies to captivate disengaged learners
  • Motivated life-long learner, particularly in the fields of education, computer science and learning science
